What is the Brix scale?
The Brix scale was created by Dr caret Reams in the 1970s. The scale is based on the sugar content within a plant. High Brix values correlate to stronger resistance to pests and disease, excellent yields, more flavor, and higher levels of biological activity in the soil - all elements associated with good nutrition - although this is yet to be rigorously tested.
